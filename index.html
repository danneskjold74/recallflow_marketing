Common questions

What kinds of businesses is RevisitIQ for?

Any small service business where customers should come back on a regular cycle — hair salons, barbers, auto shops, HVAC, cleaning services, pet groomers, and similar. RevisitIQ is recall software built for businesses that depend on repeat visits.

Do I need to replace my booking software?

No. RevisitIQ is a lightweight recall overlay, not a booking platform. It works alongside whatever you already use — it just tracks who is due back and helps you follow up.

Can I upload customers from my current system?

Yes. You can import customers via CSV upload, so you don't have to re-enter everyone by hand.

Does RevisitIQ send recall text messages?

Yes. RevisitIQ can send simple recall text messages to customers who are due back. Messages are short, practical reminders — not marketing blasts.

What happens if a customer opts out?

Customers can opt out of recall messages at any time. RevisitIQ handles opt-outs automatically so you stay compliant.
