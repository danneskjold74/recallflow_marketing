SMS Use Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

RevisitIQ is intended for simple recall, reminder, and follow-up messaging to customers a business already serves. It is not designed for bulk spam or unsolicited promotional texting.

Who this product is for

RevisitIQ is built for small service businesses — salons, auto shops, pest control companies, and similar — that want to send simple text reminders to existing customers who are due for a return visit or recurring service.

Appropriate messaging use

Messages sent through RevisitIQ should be simple, practical recall or service reminders. Appropriate messages let a customer know they are due or overdue for a service your business already provides to them.

Examples of appropriate use:

  • Reminding a customer their quarterly pest treatment is due
  • Following up with a salon client who hasn't visited in their usual cycle
  • Notifying an auto service customer that their next oil change window is coming up

Consent and existing customer relationships

You should only use RevisitIQ to message customers you have an existing business relationship with and who have a reasonable expectation of hearing from your business. Do not upload purchased lists, scraped contacts, or people who have never done business with you.

You are responsible for ensuring you have an appropriate basis to contact each recipient. If your industry or jurisdiction has specific consent requirements, consult your own legal counsel.

Opt-out handling

RevisitIQ includes opt-out handling. When a recipient replies to opt out, their preference is recorded and future messages to that number are blocked automatically.

You must honor opt-out requests. Do not attempt to re-add or re-message customers who have opted out through the system.

Message frequency and rates

RevisitIQ is designed for low-frequency, service-based messaging — not high-volume campaigns. Typical use involves occasional recall or reminder texts, not daily or weekly blasts.

Standard message and data rates may apply to recipients depending on their mobile carrier and plan.

Prohibited use

You may not use RevisitIQ to:

  • Send spam or unsolicited bulk messages
  • Message contacts from purchased, rented, or scraped lists
  • Send deceptive, misleading, or fraudulent messages
  • Harass, threaten, or send abusive content
  • Promote illegal products, services, or activities
  • Circumvent opt-out mechanisms or re-message opted-out recipients

Accounts found in violation may be suspended or terminated without notice.

Delivery limitations

Message delivery depends on third-party messaging providers and recipient mobile carriers. RevisitIQ does not guarantee delivery of any individual message. Delivery failures can occur due to invalid numbers, carrier filtering, network issues, or recipient device settings.
