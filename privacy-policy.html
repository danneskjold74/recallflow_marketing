Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

RevisitIQ is designed to help small service businesses send simple recall and follow-up messages to customers they already serve. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to users of the service.

This policy is written in plain language. It is not a substitute for legal advice. If your business has specific privacy or compliance requirements, consult your own legal counsel.

Information we collect

When you create an account, we collect basic account information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and business name.

We also collect standard usage information when you visit our website or use the app, including browser type, pages visited, and general interaction data.

Information you upload

RevisitIQ allows you to upload customer data via CSV. This typically includes customer first names, phone numbers, and service-related information such as last visit dates or service cycles. You control what data you upload.

How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the RevisitIQ service
  • Send recall and reminder text messages on your behalf
  • Process opt-out requests from message recipients
  • Improve the product and fix issues
  • Communicate with you about your account

We do not sell your data or your customers' data. We do not use uploaded customer data for our own marketing purposes.

SMS and messaging

When you use RevisitIQ to send recall or reminder messages, we transmit message content and recipient phone numbers to our messaging provider in order to deliver those messages. We also process opt-out requests from recipients who reply to unsubscribe.

Message delivery depends on third-party carriers and is not guaranteed. Standard message and data rates may apply to recipients.

Service providers

We use third-party providers to operate the service. These include providers for hosting, database and authentication services, SMS messaging, and website analytics. These providers access data only as needed to perform their functions on our behalf.

Cookies and analytics

Our marketing website uses cookies and analytics tools (including Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the site. These tools collect standard browsing data such as pages visited, time on site, and referral source. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data retention and deletion

We retain account and uploaded data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the service. If you want to delete your account or the data associated with it, contact us at hello@revisitiq.com and we will work with you to process the request.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the data stored in our systems. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your responsibilities

You are responsible for making sure you have an appropriate basis to contact the customers whose information you upload. You should only message customers you have an existing relationship with and who have not opted out of communications from your business. See our SMS Use Policy for more detail.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will note the updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, contact us at hello@revisitiq.com.
