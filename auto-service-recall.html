Due customers are easy to miss

They meant to come in for that oil change. But nobody followed up, the sticker on the windshield faded, and they ended up at the first shop that came to mind.

Customers lose track of when they are due

Most customers do not remember when their oil change, tire rotation, or inspection is due. If you don't follow up, nobody will.

No follow-up after the visit

The customer pays, drives off, and you don't hear from them until something goes wrong or they try a different shop.

Seasonal customers don't come back on their own

Winterization, AC service, brake inspections. Customers who came last year will not automatically come back this year.

Shop software doesn't do recall

Most shop systems handle estimates and invoicing. They don't help you follow up with customers who are due back.

Common questions

Do I need to replace my shop management software?

No. RevisitIQ works alongside your existing system. It handles the one thing most shop software doesn't: recall and follow-up.

Can I upload customers from my current system?

Yes. Export a CSV from your shop management system or spreadsheet and import it into RevisitIQ. No manual re-entry needed.

Can I upload customers for different types of service follow-up?

Yes. Your CSV can include customers due for oil changes, inspections, seasonal work, or any other service. You review and confirm the list before sending.

What if a customer opts out?

Customers can opt out of recall messages at any time. RevisitIQ handles opt-outs automatically so you stay compliant.
