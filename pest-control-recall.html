Common questions

Do I need to replace my pest control software?

No. RevisitIQ works alongside your existing field service tools. It handles the one thing most pest control software doesn't — following up with customers who are due for service reminders.

Can I upload customers from my current system?

Yes. Export a CSV from your current software or spreadsheet and import it into RevisitIQ. No manual re-entry needed.

Does it handle different treatment schedules?

Yes. You set the service cycle per customer — quarterly treatments, bi-monthly visits, seasonal one-time services. RevisitIQ tracks each one separately.

What if a customer opts out?

Customers can opt out of recall messages at any time. RevisitIQ handles opt-outs automatically so you stay compliant.

Is this a CRM or marketing platform?

No. RevisitIQ is a lightweight follow-up tool. It does one thing: helps you see which customers are due for treatment and send a simple reminder before they slip off schedule.
