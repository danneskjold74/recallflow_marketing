Recurring service only works when customers stay on schedule. But most people are not thinking about pest control until they see a problem. If nobody follows up, a customer who meant to stay on quarterly or seasonal service can quietly disappear.

Customers do not usually remember on their own

Most recurring customers are not tracking their next treatment date. They assume they will deal with it later. Later turns into a missed cycle, and missed cycles turn into lost revenue.

Treatment cycles lapse quietly

A quarterly customer skips one service. Then another. By the time someone notices, that account may already be cold or calling somebody else.

Seasonal customers are easy to miss

Spring, summer, and fall service windows come around fast. Customers who used you last season do not automatically come back unless someone reaches out.

Follow-up gets pushed behind everything else

Office staff are busy. Owners are juggling the whole business. Techs are in the field. The overdue list often exists somewhere, but nobody has time to work it consistently.

Your software may run the route, but not the recall

Most pest control software is built for scheduling, routing, and operations. It is not built for the simple job of taking a list of due customers and sending a straightforward recall text.

Common questions

Do I need to replace my pest control software?

No. RevisitIQ is meant to sit alongside the tools you already use. It handles a narrow recall workflow, not scheduling, routing, or broader business operations.

Can I upload customers from my current system?

Yes. RevisitIQ is built around a simple CSV upload workflow. Export your list, upload it, review the recipients, and send your recall message.

Does RevisitIQ track different treatment schedules for me?

Not automatically. You decide which customers belong in the list you upload. RevisitIQ helps you review that list and send outreach to the right people.

What if a customer opts out?

Opt-out handling is built in. Customers who opt out are recorded so you have a clear record and can avoid messaging them again through the platform.

Is this a CRM or marketing platform?

No. RevisitIQ is a simple recall tool for small service businesses. It is focused on helping you reach due customers, not replacing your main business system.
